This page will explain what the device does

The device is an accesibility for blind people when the door bell rings or when mail comes through. it does this by using the doorbells system but by adding a light to the system. so when the door bell is rung the light will flash. for the mail a motion detector will be placed at the mail box and detects when something has passed through it. A light will be activated. This light will be a differnt colour than the light for the doorbell to not confuse people about which one is which.

The light colour won't be pre determined
